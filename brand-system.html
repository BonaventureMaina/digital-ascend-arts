Usage Guidelines

Core principles for maintaining brand integrity

✓ DO

  • Maintain minimum clear space around logo
  • Use approved color combinations
  • Scale logo proportionally
  • Use icon-only version for social avatars
  • Ensure logo has sufficient contrast with background
  • Use vector formats when possible (SVG for web)

✗ DON'T

  • Stretch or distort the logo
  • Change gradient colors or direction
  • Add effects (shadows, outlines, glows)
  • Place logo on busy backgrounds
  • Separate icon from wordmark in primary logo
  • Use logo smaller than minimum size

Clear Space Rule

Maintain clear space equal to the height of the icon on all sides of the logo. This ensures the logo has breathing room and remains visually distinct from surrounding elements.
