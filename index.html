About

Digital Ascend Arts Production is an independent arts production company dedicated to the creation of original physical theatre and contemporary performance work.

We operate as an umbrella platform for artistic exploration, allowing projects to evolve organically while maintaining a clear commitment to craft, discipline, and conceptual depth.

Artistic Philosophy

We believe the body is a primary site of meaning. Movement, presence, and spatial awareness are not decorative elements, but fundamental tools for storytelling and inquiry.

Our work prioritizes clarity of intention, embodied intelligence, and rigorous process. We value exploration over spectacle and depth over excess.

Founder

Digital Ascend Arts Production was founded by Bonaventure Maina, a technologist and creative practitioner with a strong interest in the intersection of systems thinking, embodiment, and artistic process.

The company emerged from a desire to build a disciplined yet flexible platform for physical theatre and contemporary performance—one that supports long-term artistic growth rather than short-term output.
