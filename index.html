About Digital Ascend

Digital Ascend Arts Production creates a new category of theatrical entertainment: Romantic Thriller Theater.

We produce quarterly 90-minute immersive experiences where fiction, intimacy, and danger collide on stage. Our work blends the sensual sophistication of premium streaming drama with the visceral immediacy only live performance delivers.

This is entertainment-first theater for adults craving sophisticated fictional escape. No lessons. No agenda. Just story.

The Experience

Each production is an immersive 90-minute journey exploring desire, betrayal, and consequence through the lens of romantic suspense.

  • Immersive Staging
    Environments designed to pull you into the story world, not hold you at a distance
  • Sophisticated Maturity
    Bridgerton-level sensuality—suggestive and provocative, never gratuitous or explicit
  • Psychological Tension
    Character choices that keep you questioning motives, loyalties, and outcomes
  • Quarterly Productions
    New stories, same genre, consistent production quality you can depend on
Content Rating: 18+
Mature themes, sensual content, psychological intensity. Not for children. Not for moralizing. Just for you.

Why We Exist

Kenya's theatrical landscape is dominated by educational content, social commentary, and messaging-driven work. We believe there's hunger for something different: theatrical entertainment that respects adult audiences seeking escapism.

Our work prioritizes story craft, character depth, and production sophistication. We create experiences you'd want to attend quarterly, not once out of obligation.

Fiction matters. Escapism matters. Entertainment without apology matters.

Founder

Digital Ascend Arts Production was founded by Bonaventure Maina in response to a gap in East Africa's theatrical landscape: the absence of genre-specific, entertainment-first theater for adult audiences.

Combining a background in systems thinking and technology with a passion for narrative craft, the company operates as both production house and category creator—pioneering Romantic Thriller Theater as a distinct theatrical offering in the region.
